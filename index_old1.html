About Me

I am a motivated professional in machine learning, signal processing, deep learning, and computer vision, with extensive experience designing and implementing innovative algorithms in machine learning methodologies, high dimensional data representation learning, inverse problem, optimization, signal/image processing, and graph data analysis.

I have contributed to multiple NSF-funded research projects and have authored high-impact journal and conference papers in TMLR, IEEE TSP, CVPR, ICCV, and ECML. My work has also led to multiple patents in adversarial machine learning.

I am passionate about developing robust/reliable ML models and applying mathematical optimization techniques to improve data fairness, privacy, and efficiency in machine learning applications.
